## üõ°Ô∏è RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: AI Skepticism (Homeschool Parents Fear AI)
**Mitigation**: 
- Lead with Scott's story (human first, AI second)
- Emphasize parental control (you're still the teacher)
- Offer "human-only" mode (no AI, just resources)

### Risk 2: Woke Ideology Concerns
**Mitigation**:
- Explicitly state Reformed worldview
- Show examples (how we teach history, science, etc.)
- Offer refund if anyone detects leftist bias

### Risk 3: Price Sensitivity
**Mitigation**:
- Free tier for skeptics
- Pay-what-you-want option
- Scholarship fund for struggling families

### Risk 4: Content Moderation (What if AI says something wrong?)
**Mitigation**:
- Human review of controversial topics
- Parent override button ("flag this response")
- Continuous prompt refinement
