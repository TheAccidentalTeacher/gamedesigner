## ü§ñ RESEARCH EXECUTION STRATEGY

### **Option 1: Sequential Deep Research** (Slower, more thorough)
- Research one persona at a time
- 30-60 minutes per persona
- Create detailed notes before building persona file
- Total time: 8-12 hours

### **Option 2: Parallel Research Batches** (Faster, leverages AI)
- Group personas by research type
  - **Batch 1**: Education (Master Teacher, Classical Educator, Gen Alpha)
  - **Batch 2**: Technical (Architect, Debugger, UX Designer)
  - **Batch 3**: Business (Strategist, Marketing, Analyst)
  - **Batch 4**: Specialized (Theologian, Writer, Game Designer)
- Research 3-4 personas simultaneously using multiple AI instances
- Total time: 4-6 hours

### **Option 3: Hybrid Approach** (Recommended)
- Start with The 4 Core personas Scott will use most:
  1. Master Teacher
  2. Technical Architect
  3. Strategist
  4. Theologian
- Deep research these first (3-4 hours)
- Build and test them
- Then research the remaining 8 in batches (4-6 hours)
